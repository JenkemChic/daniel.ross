Project Overview

Helio is a mobile-first weather app that helps travelers plan safe routes with real-time weather updates. Users can check current conditions, forecasted weather, and route-specific warnings. A 15-minute emergency notification system warns users of sudden weather changes, supporting safer rerouting.

Problem & Research

Many travelers rely on generic weather apps that lack route-specific updates, making it harder to adjust plans on the go. Through interviews and surveys, I found that people want to see conditions not just at their current location or destination but along their entire route. They also need alerts that are clear, timely, and easy to act on.

Design & Prototyping

I created wireframes and a high-fidelity prototype in Figma, focusing on route input, clear weather icons, and short emergency alerts. I validated the design with quick user surveys and added an animated weather pattern layer to keep information visual and accessible.

Outcome & Reflection

Helio shows how clear visual design and real-time updates can make route-based weather planning safer and more user-friendly. Future improvements will include more detailed forecasting layers and integration with external navigation apps for smoother rerouting.

“I like that Helio shows weather changes before I even get there — it makes me feel safer changing my route early.” John Miller, 39 — Project Manager
