Galeriste Case Study

Galeriste is a mobile-first app concept created to help artists and patrons connect more efficiently at local art shows. Artists use the app to upload artwork, set minimum bids, and manage sales across events. Patrons enter shows by scanning a QR code and can bid on works immediately via Venmo or PayPal integration.

One key challenge addressed was making the show accessible after its physical closure, allowing bidding to continue city to city. This helped maximize exposure and sale value. Privacy and security were emphasized by wiping auctions after completion.

The result is a minimal, paper-inspired black-and-white interface allowing art to take center stage while providing artists with more autonomy and patrons with convenient access.
