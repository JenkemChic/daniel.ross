Project Overview

Galeriste is a mobile-first tool that helps artists and patrons connect at independent art shows. It lets artists create an onsite auction for pieces by generating a QR code that patrons scan to place bids. This expands audience reach, keeps the auction going after the show, and simplifies the purchase process.

Problem & Research

Independent artists often rely on single-night shows or small local events to sell their work, limiting exposure and potential sales. Patrons who can’t bid in person or need more time lose the chance to buy pieces they want. Interviews and observations with artists and buyers showed the need for an easy way to keep bidding open and accessible.

Design & Prototyping

I developed wireframes and flows in Figma, focusing on QR code generation, simple onboarding for patrons, and payment integration with PayPal or Venmo. Usability testing and feedback from artists confirmed the need for clear auction timelines, bid notifications, and simple confirmation flows.

Outcome & Reflection

Galeriste gives artists a simple tool to extend their shows into a mobile auction marketplace. Patrons can bid with just a scan and continue to compete for art after the event. Future updates will refine payment security and add features for artists to track bids across multiple shows.

“I really like the idea that I could come to the show, browse around, but still bid on something later from home if I keep thinking about it. I don’t always have the money on me or want to commit right away — so being able to leave and still place a bid is really convenient.” — Sarah L., Local Artist/Art Enthusiast (Prototype Feedback)
